About Us

Pixel Generation Labs started as a group of server owners creating our own Minecraft servers and realized how expensive it can get, especially the building portion. So we decided to band together to create a system that can help other server owners cut costs and create their own dream projects in Minecraft and share it with other people.

Our goal is simple. Provide you with the best Minecraft builds for the lowest prices on the market, so that you can easily create and share your own ideas. Helping you create amazing servers in the Minecraft community and ultimately making the game better!

Through the skill and artistic expression of our specialized builders, we utilize special tools to quickly create unique & top notch Minecraft builds that are meant to be used on your Minecraft servers.
